Boston's House of Blues


In a city of gritty rock clubs, the House of Blues is a shimmering outsider. Still, you can't beat the big name acts that parade through it's cavernous expanses. Rock- & blues-themed chain with Southern dishes such as po' boys & jambalaya, plus live music.
